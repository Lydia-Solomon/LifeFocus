Life Focus is a non-profit entity that is dedicated to helping those in need. Its primary goal is to raise funds and provide assistance to individuals, communities, or organizations that require support. These organizations work in various fields. The system can capture the details of all the donations received by Life Focus Trust that will map to various accounting heads in the organization. There will be an option to print and email the receipts as needed. The system can be used to manage all donor and beneficiary registry of the Life Focus Trust.
